Energy Warriors Challenge

Win up to USD 100,000

1st March – 1st May 2020

The Energy Warriors challenge requires you to arrive at sustainability strategy and compliance management system that applies to all end-use energy consumption: power, heat, and transport.
You can find a solution either for the supply side (energy generation for end-use energy consumption) or demand side (including energy efficiency) programs, or both.

You must demonstrate applicability, impact, sustainability and scalability in supporting renewable energy transformation to achieve 100% renewables at local, regional or global scales. 

Your solution must

• Clearly demonstrate that your solution can enable a transformation to
100% renewable energy
• Be replicable, scalable and sustainable
• Be suitable for use in developing countries


About Energy Warriors Challenge

03.

04.

07.
